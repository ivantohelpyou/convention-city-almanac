# Taipei

The distributed participation model. Night markets, community centers, and a cultural creative park — connected by metro, not adjacency.

## Facilities

- **[Taipei Nangang Exhibition Center (TaiNEX)](/cities/taipei/convention-center/)** — Nangang. Opened 2008 (Hall 1), 2019 (Hall 2). *~460,000 sq ft* exhibit. Walk Score *~75 estimated*.

## Participation Infrastructure

- **[Songshan Cultural and Creative Park](/cities/taipei/songshan/)** — Xinyi District. Former tobacco factory. Studios, workshops, exhibitions, performance. 5km from TaiNEX — connected by metro, not adjacent.
- **Raohe Street Night Market** — *~2.5km* from TaiNEX. Nightly vendor participation.
- **Nangang community centers (活動中心)** — District-level civic participation, classes, senior programming.
