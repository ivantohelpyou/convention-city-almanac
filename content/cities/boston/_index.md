# Boston

Two convention facilities in different neighborhoods — 1.5 miles apart. The geographic separation drove the Hynes sale fight.

## Facilities

- **[Hynes Convention Center](/cities/boston/hynes/)** — Back Bay. Opened 1988. 176,480 sq ft. Walk Score 97. The neighborhood fought to keep it.
- **[BCEC / Menino Center](/cities/boston/bcec/)** — Seaport. Opened 2004. 516,000 sq ft. Walk Score 62. Expansion on hold.
