## The Robotics Advantage

Pittsburgh is the only city besides Boston with a strong Robotics score (2.1). The [CMU Robotics Innovation Center](/cities/pittsburgh/cmu-robotics/) opened in 2026 — 150,000 sq ft of research and prototyping space on the former J&L Steel site at Hazelwood Green. The university-anchored model differs from Boston's nonprofit model ([MassRobotics](/cities/boston/massrobotics/)), but both produce daily activation of large space.

Combined Robotics + Education = 5.3 — reflecting Pittsburgh's university-driven participation model.
