## Why London Ties New York

London matches NYC at 38 through different strengths: **City Lit** (5,000+ adult education courses — nothing comparable in the US), **lidos** (outdoor swimming pools as civic infrastructure), **Parkrun** (invented here in 2004, now in 22 countries — the world's most successful participation sport innovation), and fringe theater at a scale that rivals off-off-Broadway.

ExCeL (1M sq ft) and Olympia together host 500+ events/year — comparable to RAI Amsterdam for Industry Networking.
