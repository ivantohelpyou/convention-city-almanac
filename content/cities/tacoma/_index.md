# Tacoma

Convention center and arena under one city department, 20 miles from Sea-Tac, on the Sounder line. Future Link light rail connection (2035).

## Facilities

- **[Greater Tacoma Convention Center](/cities/tacoma/convention-center/)** — 1500 Commerce Street. Opened 2004. ~119,000 sq ft event space. Walk Score 87.
- **Tacoma Dome** — 2727 East D Street. Opened 1983. 5,000–23,000 capacity. Largest indoor venue in Washington State. $32M renovation 2018.

Both operated by **Tacoma Venues & Events** (City of Tacoma). Connected by T Line light rail.
